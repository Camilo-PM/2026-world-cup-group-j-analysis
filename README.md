# FIFA World Cup 2026 - Group J Team Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the recent performance of the teams competing in Group J of the FIFA World Cup 2026 using data analysis and visualization techniques.

The study evaluates the last 10 official and friendly matches played by each national team to identify:

* Current form
* Offensive and defensive efficiency
* Win consistency
* Goal production
* Defensive solidity
* Overall competitive strength

The project was developed using Python, pandas and Tableau to generate statistical insights and interactive visualizations.

## Main Findings

### Austria

* Highest Power Score in the group
* Most productive attack
* Excellent tactical balance between offense and defense

### Argentina

* Best defensive record in the group
* Extremely consistent performances
* One of the strongest overall teams statistically

### Algeria

* Strong offensive transitions
* High win percentage
* Potential surprise contender of the group

### Jordan

* Lowest Power Score
* Defensive vulnerabilities against stronger opponents
* Significant gap compared to the top three teams
